ABOUT

       You are Darthax, a cosmic snake, consumer of planets. The abundance of Planets in your galaxy
poses a serious threat to the universe. Planets would collide, releasing great amounts of energy,
leading to catastrophic destruction. You were sent to this galaxy to consume its planets while weaving
through asteroid fields in order to maintain equilibrium in our universe.

TIPS AND TRICKS:

       Plan your moves ahead to avoid getting trapped. Use obstacles to your advantage by maneuvering the snake around them strategically. Be cautious when the snake becomes longer, as it becomes easier to collide with obstacles or itself.
